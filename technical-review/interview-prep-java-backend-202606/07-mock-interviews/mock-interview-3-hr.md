# Mock Interview 3 — HR / Behavioral

## Mục tiêu

Chuẩn bị cho HR và hiring manager behavioral round. Tập trung vào motivation, communication, ownership, teamwork, salary.

---

## Q1 [Easy] — Tell me about yourself.

**Gợi ý trả lời:** xem [01-cv-self-introduction.md](../01-cv-self-introduction.md).

## Q2 [Easy] — Why are you looking for a new job?

**Answer outline:**

- Growth in Java backend/system design.
- More ownership/impact.
- Avoid negative comments.

## Q3 [Medium] — What are your strengths?

**Answer outline:**

- Java/Spring backend depth.
- Distributed systems/cloud experience.
- Ownership and code quality.
- Collaboration with international teams.

## Q4 [Medium] — What is your weakness?

**Answer outline:**

- Used to over-engineer or aim for too complete solution.
- Improved by incremental delivery and early alignment.

## Q5 [Medium] — Describe a conflict with a teammate.

**Answer outline STAR:**

- Situation: disagreement on design.
- Task: choose solution.
- Action: compare trade-offs with facts.
- Result: aligned decision.

## Q6 [Hard] — Tell me about a production issue you handled.

**Answer outline STAR:**

- Situation: alert/error.
- Task: reduce impact.
- Action: inspect metrics/logs, mitigate, communicate.
- Result: service recovered, prevention added.

## Q7 [Medium] — How do you handle pressure near release deadline?

**Answer outline:**

- Prioritize critical issues.
- Communicate risk early.
- Avoid unsafe shortcuts for banking/security-critical parts.
- Use feature flag/rollback plan if possible.

## Q8 [Medium] — How do you mentor junior engineers?

**Answer outline:**

- Review with explanation.
- Pair programming.
- Give context, not only instruction.
- Encourage ownership gradually.

## Q9 [Hard] — If Product wants fast release but QA finds serious bug, what do you do?

**Answer outline:**

- Assess severity and user/business impact.
- Communicate clearly with Product/QA/Tech lead.
- If critical: block release or disable feature.
- If acceptable: document known issue, mitigation, follow-up fix.
- For banking/financial/security, lean conservative.

## Q10 [Medium] — Expected salary?

**Answer outline:**

- Ask about scope/total package if early.
- Provide range if required.
- Mention flexibility based on role, growth, benefits.

## Q11 [Hard] — If we offer less than your expectation, would you accept?

**Answer outline:**

> Em sẽ cân nhắc dựa trên total package, scope công việc, growth opportunity và technical challenge. Compensation quan trọng, nhưng em cũng quan tâm long-term fit. Nếu package chưa đạt expectation, em mong có thể discuss thêm để tìm mức phù hợp cho cả hai bên.

## Q12 [Easy] — Do you have any questions for us?

Nên hỏi:

- Team structure.
- Technical challenges.
- Expectation for senior role.
- Engineering culture.
- Growth path.

## Checklist tự luyện

- Trả lời trong 1–2 phút/câu.
- Không nói xấu công ty cũ.
- Có ví dụ cụ thể.
- Có kết quả đo được nếu có.
- Giữ thái độ senior: calm, structured, ownership.
