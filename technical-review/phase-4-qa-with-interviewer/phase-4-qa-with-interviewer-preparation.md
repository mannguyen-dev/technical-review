# Phase 4: Q&A with Interviewer Preparation

## 1. Overview

Phase 4 là phần bạn đặt câu hỏi lại cho interviewer. Nhiều ứng viên xem đây là phần phụ, nhưng với vị trí **Senior Java Developer / Backend Engineer tại NAB**, đây là cơ hội để thể hiện:

- Bạn có chuẩn bị nghiêm túc cho buổi phỏng vấn.
- Bạn quan tâm đến role, team và long-term contribution.
- Bạn có mindset Senior Engineer: engineering quality, ownership, architecture, production reliability.
- Bạn hiểu đặc thù digital banking: security, reliability, auditability, data correctness.
- Bạn muốn hiểu cách team làm việc, không chỉ quan tâm lương/phúc lợi.

Interviewer có thể đánh giá qua câu hỏi của bạn:

- Bạn có hiểu role mình apply không.
- Bạn có suy nghĩ như người sẽ join team và contribute thật không.
- Bạn có quan tâm đến quality/process/architecture hay chỉ muốn một công việc bất kỳ.
- Bạn có communication tự nhiên, chuyên nghiệp không.

Cách tiếp cận tốt:

```text
Ask about expectation → Ask about system/team → Ask about engineering practices → Ask about growth/next steps
```

Không nên hỏi quá nhiều. Nếu thời gian ngắn, hãy chọn **3–5 câu hỏi tốt nhất**.

---

## 2. Principles for Asking Good Questions

- Ưu tiên câu hỏi liên quan đến **role expectation**, **team**, **architecture**, **engineering practices**.
- Luôn có ít nhất 1 câu hỏi về expectation trong **3–6 tháng đầu**.
- Hỏi về backend architecture, tech stack, service communication, data consistency nếu interviewer là technical lead/senior engineer.
- Hỏi về collaboration giữa backend, frontend, QA, BA, PO để thể hiện bạn hiểu Agile/cross-functional delivery.
- Hỏi về quality, testing, CI/CD, code review, production support để thể hiện senior mindset.
- Hỏi về banking domain nếu phù hợp: reliability, auditability, critical flows, customer impact.
- Không hỏi những thông tin quá dễ tìm trên website/JD.
- Không mở đầu bằng lương/phúc lợi/remote/overtime nếu chưa đến vòng HR hoặc offer.
- Không hỏi theo kiểu chất vấn hoặc tiêu cực.
- Nên hỏi tự nhiên, có context ngắn trước câu hỏi.

Câu mở đầu gợi ý:

```text
Yes, thank you. I would like to understand more about the role, the team, and the engineering practices.
```

Nếu thời gian ít:

```text
I have a few questions, but I will prioritize the most important ones.
```

## 3. Top 5 Questions to Prioritize

| No. | Question | Why ask this |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | What are the main expectations for this role in the first 3 to 6 months? | Thể hiện bạn quan tâm đến success criteria và muốn contribute thực tế |
| 2 | Could you share more about the backend architecture and the main technologies used by the team? | Cho thấy bạn quan tâm technical fit và có mindset backend |
| 3 | How does the team balance delivery speed with code quality and production reliability? | Rất hợp môi trường banking; thể hiện senior mindset |
| 4 | How do backend developers collaborate with frontend, QA, BA, and product teams? | Cho thấy bạn hiểu delivery là cross-functional |
| 5 | Based on our discussion today, is there anything about my background that you would like me to clarify further? | Câu closing mạnh để xử lý concern ngay trong interview |

Nếu interviewer là engineering manager, ưu tiên câu 1, 3, 4, 5.  
Nếu interviewer là tech lead/senior engineer, ưu tiên câu 2, 3, CI/CD/production support và architecture.

## 4. Question Bank by Category

## 4.1 Role Expectation

### Question 1: What are the main expectations for this role in the first 3 to 6 months?

#### Why this is a good question

Câu này rất nên hỏi vì nó thể hiện bạn muốn hiểu rõ expectation và chuẩn bị để đóng góp sớm. Với Senior role, interviewer sẽ thấy bạn không chỉ muốn pass interview mà còn quan tâm đến success sau khi join.

#### What it helps you understand

- Team mong đợi bạn deliver gì trong giai đoạn đầu.
- Role thiên về feature delivery, production support, modernization hay architecture.
- Bạn cần chuẩn bị thêm kỹ năng gì nếu join.

#### When to ask

Nên hỏi đầu Phase 4, đặc biệt nếu interviewer là hiring manager, tech lead hoặc engineering manager.

#### Vietnamese meaning

Trong 3–6 tháng đầu, team kỳ vọng gì chính ở vị trí này?

#### Optional follow-up

```text
Are there any specific projects or challenges that the new joiner would likely work on first?
```

---

### Question 2: What would success look like for this position after the probation period?

#### Why this is a good question

Câu này giúp bạn hiểu tiêu chí đánh giá performance. Nó cũng thể hiện bạn quan tâm đến outcome, không chỉ task.

#### What it helps you understand

- Success metrics cho role.
- Kỳ vọng về delivery, ownership, communication.
- Mức độ seniority team mong đợi.

#### When to ask

Hỏi sau câu expectation hoặc nếu interviewer nói về onboarding/probation.

#### Vietnamese meaning

Sau probation, thế nào được xem là thành công cho vị trí này?

#### Optional follow-up

```text
Is success measured more by feature delivery, production ownership, technical leadership, or a combination of these?
```

---

### Question 3: What are the most important qualities you expect from a Senior Java Developer in this team?

#### Why this is a good question

Câu này giúp bạn hiểu team đang cần senior kiểu nào: technical depth, ownership, communication, fullstack, mentoring hay production support.

#### What it helps you understand

- Team value gì ở Senior Engineer.
- Kỹ năng nào cần nhấn mạnh nếu có vòng sau.
- Culture về ownership và collaboration.

#### When to ask

Phù hợp khi nói chuyện với tech lead hoặc engineering manager.

#### Vietnamese meaning

Team kỳ vọng phẩm chất quan trọng nhất nào ở một Senior Java Developer?

#### Optional follow-up

```text
From your experience, what makes someone successful in this team?
```

## 5. Questions to Avoid or Ask Carefully

| Sensitive Topic | Avoid Asking | Better Way to Ask |
|---|---|---|
| Salary | `What is the salary for this role?` | `I am happy to discuss compensation with HR at the appropriate stage. For now, I would like to understand more about the role and expectations.` |
| Benefits | `What benefits do I get?` | `Could you share where I can find more information about the full benefits package, or should I discuss that with HR in the next stage?` |
| Overtime | `Do you often work overtime?` | `How does the team usually manage workload and release pressure during critical delivery periods?` |
| Workload | `Is the workload heavy?` | `How does the team plan sprint capacity and handle urgent production or business priorities?` |
| Remote policy | `Can I work from home all the time?` | `Could you share the team's current working model and how collaboration works in that setup?` |
| Promotion | `How soon can I get promoted?` | `What does the career growth path typically look like for Senior Engineers in the team?` |
| Easy website info | `What does NAB do?` | `I understand NAB has a strong digital banking focus. Could you share how this team contributes to that strategy?` |
| Negative team culture | `Is the team stressful?` | `How would you describe the engineering culture and the way the team supports each other during busy periods?` |
| Technology criticism | `Why are you still using old technology?` | `Are there any modernization initiatives or technology migration plans in the team?` |
| On-call concern | `Do I have to be on-call?` | `Are developers involved in production support or incident response, and how is that rotation usually managed?` |

Nguyên tắc: nếu câu hỏi có thể nghe như “đòi quyền lợi” hoặc “sợ việc”, hãy chuyển thành câu hỏi về **process, collaboration, expectation, support model**.

## 6. Closing Statement

Bạn có thể kết thúc interview bằng đoạn ngắn sau:

```text
Thank you for taking the time to speak with me today. I really appreciate the opportunity to learn more about the role and the team.

From our discussion, I feel this position is well aligned with my Java backend experience, especially my background in banking systems, microservices, event-driven architecture, and production support.

I am very interested in the opportunity to contribute to NAB's digital banking systems and continue growing with the team. Please let me know if there is any additional information I can provide after this interview.
```

Nếu muốn ngắn hơn:

```text
Thank you for your time today. I enjoyed learning more about the role and the team. Based on our discussion, I feel my Java backend, banking, microservices, and production support experience align well with what you are looking for. I would be very happy to continue with the next steps.
```
